My Computer Science Experience

Coming into the computer science program at George Fox University, I had no previous computer science experience. I had not taken any courses or even had a full understanding of what was encompassed within "Computer Science".

The first computer science course that I took at George Fox was Intro to Computer Science where I learned the basics of computer science in the Python language. I immediately fell in love with the course. The course flew by and I never felt that I was doing schoolwork as I loved working on the projects. As I moved into my second course, I began to learn about object-oriented programming and while it took me a bit of time to understand the concept, once I did, it just made such perfect sense.

The computer science program at George Fox University took me from understanding nothing about computer science and taught me everything that I know today to the point where I was able to work an internship at Micron and have been hired to work there after graduation. The program taught me how to do the best that I can and always strive to improve my code no matter what it is that I'm working on.

Why I love computer science: I ended up choosing to pursue computer science because I love to problem solve. The aspect of computer science that I love the most is being able to look at a problem as a whole and bring together all the pieces to create the best possible sollution. The computer science program taught me all the best ways to do this and set me up for success.
